About DDS

Digital Defence System (DDS) is a next-generation cybersecurity platform designed to protect users from sophisticated cyber attacks. Our AI-powered threat detection engine analyzes login patterns, device fingerprints, and IP addresses to identify potential security breaches.

With our revolutionary panic button feature, users can instantly lock down their accounts during an active attack, terminating all sessions and invalidating access tokens in real-time.
