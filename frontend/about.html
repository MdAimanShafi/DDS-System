About DDS

Digital Defence System - Next-Generation Cybersecurity Platform

Our Mission

DDS (Digital Defence System) is a cutting-edge cybersecurity platform designed to protect users from sophisticated cyber attacks through real-time threat detection and instant response capabilities.

Our AI-powered risk engine analyzes login patterns, device fingerprints, IP addresses, and behavioral patterns to identify potential security breaches before they cause damage.

Key Technologies

✓ Node.js & Express.js - High-performance backend architecture
✓ MongoDB - Scalable NoSQL database for attack logs
✓ Socket.IO - Real-time bidirectional communication
✓ JWT & bcrypt - Enterprise-grade authentication
✓ Leaflet.js - Interactive attack visualization
✓ AI Risk Engine - Intelligent threat assessment
